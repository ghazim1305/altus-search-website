About Altus Search

A specialist recruitment consultancy combining market knowledge, personal service and long-term relationships.

Our approach

Advice grounded in experience and delivered with integrity.

Altus Search supports organisations and professionals across specialist front-office and corporate-governance markets in Switzerland and Europe.

Our consultants draw upon deep recruitment experience when advising clients and candidates. We invest time in understanding the context behind each requirement, from business strategy and team dynamics to technical capability and long-term career goals.

That focused approach allows us to provide a personal service built around clarity, discretion and transparent communication.

The candidates of today may become the clients of tomorrow.

Every conversation matters, and every relationship deserves respect.
