Partnership programme

Swiss recruitment partnership programme

Placement support for international recruitment agencies working on permanent opportunities in Switzerland.

International agency support

A local route for Swiss permanent placements.

Altus Search’s existing website states that recruitment agencies without a SECO placement licence, particularly those based outside Switzerland, may be prohibited from directly arranging commercial placements related to Switzerland.

It also states that the Altus Search Zug branch is authorised to facilitate permanent placements into Switzerland through its SECO placement licence, and may carry out qualifying placements on behalf of non-Swiss recruitment agencies in return for an agreed fee.

Legal review required: This page is a marketing draft, not legal advice. All statements about Swiss licensing, the AVG, fee-sharing, regulatory conditions and agency responsibilities must be reviewed by qualified Swiss legal or recruitment-compliance professionals before publication.

Frequently asked questions

Questions for international recruitment partners.

Who is the programme intended for?

International or non-Swiss recruitment agencies that identify permanent placement opportunities connected with Switzerland and require local licensed support.

Does Altus Search provide legal advice?

No. Agencies should obtain independent legal and regulatory advice regarding their circumstances.

How are commercial terms agreed?

Terms should be documented in a written agreement after the opportunity and responsibilities have been reviewed.
