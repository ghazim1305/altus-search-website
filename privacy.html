Privacy policy

A structured placeholder for a legally reviewed Swiss and European data-protection notice.

Important: This is a non-legal placeholder created for the website build. It must be replaced or approved by qualified legal and data-protection professionals before launch.

1. Who controls your information

Insert the complete and verified legal identity, registered addresses, commercial register details, responsible contact and any representative or data-protection contact required by applicable law.

2. Information collected

Describe candidate, client, website, enquiry, communications and recruitment information collected through the website and other channels.

3. Purposes and legal grounds

Explain each processing purpose and applicable legal basis, including recruitment services, candidate representation, client services, compliance, administration, analytics and communications.

4. Candidate information

Describe how CVs, career histories, references, compensation information, identification details and special-category information are handled.

5. Sharing and international transfers

Identify categories of recipients, client disclosures, service providers, group companies, authorities and safeguards for international transfers.

6. Retention

Provide specific retention periods or clear criteria for each data category.

7. Your rights

Explain access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, portability, consent withdrawal and complaint rights as applicable.

8. Security

Describe proportionate technical and organisational measures without publishing sensitive operational security details.

9. Cookies and analytics

List tools only after they are actually configured, and connect this section to a consent-management system.

10. Updates and contact

Include the effective date, revision process and verified privacy contact details.
